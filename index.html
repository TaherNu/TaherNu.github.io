WE ARE VALORANT

Valorant is a 5v5 first shooter game, You have 13 rounds to attack and defend your side using guns and agent abilities. And, with one life per-round, you'll need to think faster than your opponent if you wish to survive. Modes include: Competitive play, Unrated, deathmatch, team-deathmatch, spike Rush, and Swift Play

YOUR AGENTS

More than just guns and bullets, you have the option to choose an agent which has their own adaptive, swift, and deadly abilities that can create oppurtunities to let your gunplay shine. No two Agents play alike

FIGHT AROUND THE WORLD
